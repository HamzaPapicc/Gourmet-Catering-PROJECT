About Catering

Our long lasting tradition

We at Gourmet au Catering have a long history of sercing our customers, we have a high reputation and are well respected even among the most popular chefs around the world. We were founded in January 10th 1955 and to this day we remain. We have a variety of dishes, we serve breakfast, lunch and dinner, You are also able to host any kind of formal event at our restaurant. As for the cost of our services you needn't worry, all of our services are priced reasonably and you are guaranteed high quality. Our dishes are prepared with the most high quality and fresh indgridients, though we do only use seasonal ingridients.
